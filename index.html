Get started

Up and running in 60 seconds

Download, drag, done. Meetflow handles the rest.

1

Download

Click the Download button in the top nav to grab the latest DMG for your Mac.

2

Drag to Applications

Open the DMG and drag Meetflow into your Applications folder. Standard macOS install.

3

Launch & record

Open Meetflow from Applications and grant Screen Recording when asked. The speech model downloads once, then everything runs on your Mac.

100% local. Everything runs on your Mac — no cloud, no account, no telemetry. The speech recognition model downloads once on first launch and stays on your machine forever.

First launch: macOS may ask you to confirm the app is safe to open (standard for apps distributed outside the App Store). Right-click Meetflow in Applications → Open, then click Open in the dialog. One time only.
